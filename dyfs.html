DYFS Tracker

Find your Wallet-Card tracker from your Android phone using Bluetooth Low Energy, buzzer alerts, and last-known location support.

DOWNLOAD APP VIEW DETAILS

Tracker Features

Designed for a simple daily flow: connect, ping, find, and return to your day.

Find Mode

Trigger the tracker buzzer from the DYFS app when your Wallet-Card is nearby but hidden.

BLE Connection

Uses Bluetooth Low Energy to connect your Android phone with the Wallet-Card tracker.

Last Location

The app can remember the last phone location near the tracker when the connection is lost.
